The Entrepreneurial Spirit: BongiNala Logistics

In 2022, alongside his corporate responsibilities, Sifiso launched BongiNala Logistics. This venture, named in honor of two of his children, Bonga and Nala, quickly grew from a passionate idea into a thriving trucking and logistics company. BongiNala stands as a testament to his entrepreneurial drive, resilience, and ability to build successful enterprises from the ground up.

Operating across South Africa and into sub-Saharan countries, BongiNala serves major clients like Cashbuild and Builders Warehouse, transporting essential goods such as steel and cement. More than a business, BongiNala is a vehicle for empowerment, creating jobs and contributing to the economic vitality of the regions it serves. Sifiso continues to steer BongiNala's growth, balancing its demands with his corporate leadership role.
